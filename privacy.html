1. Overview

Cleber_Costa ("the App") is a personal automation tool operated by a single individual (the Owner) for use on his own TikTok account. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App processes and how that data is handled.

2. No Third-Party Users

The App does not offer registration, login, or any service to third parties. Only the Owner authenticates with his own TikTok account. No other person's personal data is collected, stored, or processed by the App.

3. Data Processed

To operate, the App processes the following data of the Owner only:

  • TikTok access token and basic profile information obtained via the TikTok Login Kit (scope user.info.basic), used to identify the authenticated account before publishing content.
  • Video files and captions generated locally by the Owner and uploaded to TikTok via the Content Posting API (scopes video.upload and video.publish).
  • Operational logs stored locally on the Owner's machine, containing timestamps, video IDs, and request/response metadata for debugging purposes.

4. How Data Is Used

The data above is used solely to authenticate the Owner with TikTok and to publish the Owner's own videos to his own TikTok account. Data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising or profiling.

5. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens and operational logs are stored locally on the Owner's machine, protected by the operating system's standard access controls. The App does not use cloud databases or external storage providers.

6. Data Retention

Access tokens are retained only while valid and are refreshed or discarded as required by TikTok. Operational logs are retained for up to 30 days and then rotated. Published video metadata remains on TikTok according to TikTok's own policies.

7. Third-Party Services

The App interacts with the following third-party services on behalf of the Owner only: TikTok for Developers (Login Kit and Content Posting API) and the Shopee Affiliate Program (for generating affiliate links). The privacy policies of those services apply to their respective platforms.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for and is not used by children under the age of 18.

9. Your Rights

Since the App processes data of the Owner only, no external user rights apply. The Owner can revoke the App's access to his TikTok account at any time through the TikTok security settings.

10. Changes to This Policy

The Owner may update this Privacy Policy. The most recent version will always be available at this URL with an updated effective date.

11. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to cleber.ifc@gmail.com.
